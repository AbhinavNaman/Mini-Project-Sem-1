Channeling Waste Water to Generate Electricity.

Created by: CSD SEM-01 Abhinav Naman

EXPECTED OUTCOMES

Energy procurement

Optimum energy procurement and utilization is achieved The treated water is safe to reuse and recycle after harmful material have been removed.

Quality of water

Pollutants, toxicants, coarse particles, pathogens are removed from the water so that the quality of water is reached to a permissible level.

Energy cost reduced

Energy cost for the whole water treatment process is reduced without compromising with the quality of treatment of water.

Waste water treatment is a great source for generation of electricity and is a great innovation and addition for the idea of sustainable development.

Waste water treatment is a high energy consuming process, so by generating electricity through waste treatment for the powerplant and other societal uses, we are reducing the pressure on fossil fuels.

Apart from generation of energy, water treatment also takes place in the powerplant which is a very important step before discharging the waste water to larger bodies as the the water being discharged is not toxic in nature.

So two important steps for enviornment care are taken by the treatment of waste water in waste water treatment powerplant
